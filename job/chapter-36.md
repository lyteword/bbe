# Job 36 

**ﾂｹ** And Elihu went on to say,

**ﾂｲ** Give me a little more time, and I will make it clear to you; for I have still something to say for God.

**ﾂｳ** I will get my knowledge from far, and I will give righteousness to my Maker.

**竅ｴ** For truly my words are not false; one who has all knowledge is talking with you.

**竅ｵ** Truly, God gives up the hard-hearted, and will not give life to the sinner.

**竅ｶ** His eyes are ever on the upright, and he gives to the crushed their right;

**竅ｷ** Lifting them up to the seat of kings, and making them safe for ever.

**竅ｸ** And if they have been prisoned in chains, and taken in cords of trouble,

**竅ｹ** Then he makes clear to them what they have done, even their evil works in which they have taken pride.

**ﾂｹ竅ｰ** Their ear is open to his teaching, and he gives them orders so that their hearts may be turned from evil.

**ﾂｹﾂｹ** If they give ear to his voice, and do his word, then he gives them long life, and years full of pleasure.

**ﾂｹﾂｲ** But if not, they come to their end, and give up their breath without knowledge.

**ﾂｹﾂｳ** Those who have no fear of God keep wrath stored up in their hearts; they give no cry for help when they are made prisoners.

**ﾂｹ竅ｴ** They come to their end while they are still young, their life is short like that of those who are used for sex purposes in the worship of their gods.

**ﾂｹ竅ｵ** He makes the wrong done to the poor the way of their salvation, opening their ears by their trouble.

**ﾂｲﾂｹ** Take care not to be turned to sin, for you have taken evil for your part in place of sorrow.

**ﾂｲﾂｲ** Truly God is lifted up in strength; who is a ruler like him?

**ﾂｲﾂｳ** Who ever gave orders to him, or said to him, You have done wrong?

**ﾂｲ竅ｴ** See that you give praise to his work, about which men make songs.

**ﾂｲ竅ｵ** All people are looking on it; man sees it from far.

**ﾂｲ竅ｶ** Truly, God is great, greater than all our knowledge; the number of his years may not be searched out.

**ﾂｲ竅ｷ** For he takes up the drops from the sea; he sends them through his mist as rain,

**ﾂｲ竅ｸ** Flowing down from the sky, and dropping on the peoples.

**ﾂｲ竅ｹ** And who has knowledge of how the clouds are stretched out, or of the thunders of his tent?

**ﾂｳ竅ｰ** See, he is stretching out his mist, covering the tops of the mountains with it.

**ﾂｳﾂｹ** For by these he gives food to the peoples, and bread in full measure.

**ﾂｳﾂｲ** He takes the light in his hands, sending it against the mark.

**ﾂｳﾂｳ** The thunder makes clear his passion, and the storm gives news of his wrath.
